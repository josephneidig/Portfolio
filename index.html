About Me

I'm a freshman undergraduate majoring in Computer Science at the University of Central Florida, currently working as a Techranger for UCF. As a Techranger, my work mainly focuses on web development, but I am also cultivating skills by studying artificial intelligence, game design, and algorithm design. Outside of my studies and work, I enjoy spending my time as a casual graphic designer, video editor, competitive gamer, and community organizer.
